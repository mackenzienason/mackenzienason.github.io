Form Finder

Make finding a winner easy...ier

Form Finders allows users to find runners (horses) based on the form factors that matters to them, without spending excessive time scanning form.  It’s a tool for the modern punter.

I was approached by the product manager to lead the design of an update to Form Finder that enhanced the current experience.  

Discovery

After collecting user feedback and talking directly with our users we noted among many requests, a re-occuring demand for a way to apply a selection of filters, and to have them easily accesible, when needed.

Introducing Presets

Through many iterations of wireframing and co-design feedback sessions we came to a single solution we identified as ‘Presets’

Presets allowed us to have a single system, not only for Form finder, but across tools and platforms that allowed users to save, remove and update collections of ‘filters’ or ‘settings’.

Aligning components

As a result of the updates to Form Finder we needed to do an audit of all current filters and controls across platforms (iOS, Android & Web) and align the components to offer a consistant and familiar experience.
